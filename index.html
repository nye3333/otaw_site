Research interests

Current research interests

My current research centres on geometric group theory, particularly on determining the JSJ decompositions of graphs of free groups with cyclic edge groups.

Prior research interests

Previously, my research focused on the geometric and algebraic properties of relatively hyperbolic and diffuse groups, building on MFoCS work with Dr Sam Hughes.
